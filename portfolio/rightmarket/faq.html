RightMarket Help Center

Top frequently asked questions

Where do you get your data from?

We source our data from Equifax who are a world leading consumer data provider.

How often is your data cleansed?

A complete flush of data occurs 4 times a year with monthly cleansing against Mail Preference Service (MPS), deceased and suppressions.

How often is your database updated?

We receive and import quarterly updates from Equifax.

How many prospects do you currently hold?

We hold approximately 38 million individual named UK consumers across just over 200 million UK households.

Are there any restrictions to how many lists I can buy?

You can buy up to 5 million records. If you are a higher volume data user, then we can accommodate that following a few simple questions.

Are my credit card details secured with you?

We use SagePay to process any credit or debit card transaction and all SagePay's standard security features apply. We do not actually hold any of your credit or debit card information.

How much does it cost to use your system?

Our system is free for you to sign up and use. You will only get charged for the mailing lists that you buy.

I have been asked by a recipient to stop sending more mails, what do I have to do?

We provide the facility to exclude certain households from the list you buy. This is accessed through the "My Suppressions" link available once you are logged in. Suppression lists can be applied each and every time you buy a list and the individuals at the excluded addresses will not be included in the list you download.

I have downloaded a list for my local area and selected filters to match my own information, but my name is not there. Does this mean your database is missing information?

While we cannot guarantee that our database will cover 100% of UK prospects, we can assure you that our database is sourced from one of the most respected data providers in the world. A possible explanation for your missing record could be that the prospect has registered on the Mail Preference Service and hence their details would be excluded. The prospect could also have contacted us directly to ask for their details to be removed which would explain why their household is not included.
